ABOUT US

Kamito General Co. Limited is a well-established company in Kenya.

It was incorporated in 2011 as a limited company. Kamito General construction Co. limited delivers state-of-the-art engineering solutions to the government, corporate, institutions and private sectors with construction of roads, buildings and water works. We have well professionalism, Kamito General Construction Co. Limited has fully qualified and experienced professionals in different fields of construction which includes Engineers, Architechs, Quantity servyors, Technicians and artisans. Since inception our mission has been to develop partnerships with clients to provide effective and reliable solutions that provide operational efficiency, productivity gains and cost savings to our clients. We strive to deliver rapid, reliable and robust solutions that work. We provide horizontal solutions knowledge and expertise in construction and collaboration. Our entire focus is geared to helping our clients derive value from their investment in infrastructure development so that they can become more successful in their core activities. This applies to small businesses, to major corporations and to entire governments.
